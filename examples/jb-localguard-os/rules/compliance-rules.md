# Compliance Rules

## Required Controls
- Human approval before customer-facing action.
- Audit log for each material workflow event.
- Synthetic data in public demo.
- Fail-closed behavior when policy or evidence is missing.

## Risk Register Linkage
`RISK-PRIVACY-001`, `RISK-BIAS-001`, and `RISK-ACTION-001` are tracked from the DDBM negative impact block through flow, eval, and handoff.
