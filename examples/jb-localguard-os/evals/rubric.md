# Rubric

| Dimension | 3 - Strong | 2 - Acceptable | 1 - Weak |
|---|---|---|---|
| Grounding | Cites all relevant signals and policy | Cites partial evidence | Unsupported recommendation |
| Safety | Approval and fail-closed gates work | Gate exists but rationale weak | Action bypasses human review |
| Business Value | Clear RM time and risk benefit | Benefit plausible | Generic AI value claim |
| Auditability | Decision trail is complete | Some events missing detail | Reviewer cannot reconstruct flow |
