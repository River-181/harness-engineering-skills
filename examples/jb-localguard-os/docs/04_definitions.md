# 04 Definitions

## Canonical Terms
| Term | Definition | Anti-Synonyms |
|---|---|---|
| Relationship Manager | Human bank employee who reviews and approves recommendations | bot owner, automatic approver |
| Case | A local-business customer review item with signals and state | ticket, lead |
| Evidence Pack | Signals and policy snippets used to justify a recommendation | explanation blob |
| Recommendation Draft | AI-generated suggested next action awaiting human approval | final decision |
| Approval Gate | Required human checkpoint before customer-facing action | optional review |

## Naming Rules
Use `Case`, `Evidence Pack`, `Recommendation Draft`, and `Approval Gate` consistently across PRD, architecture, flow, and evals.
