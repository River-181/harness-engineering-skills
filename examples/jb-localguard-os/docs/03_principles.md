# 03 Principles

## Product Principles
| ID | Principle | Enforcement |
|---|---|---|
| P-001 | Assist, do not autonomously act | All flows stop at RM approval |
| P-002 | Evidence before recommendation | Recommendation card requires cited signals |
| P-003 | Fail closed on missing policy | If policy rules are missing, action draft is blocked |
| P-004 | Synthetic public demo data | No real customer details in demo artifacts |

## Rejection Rules
- Reject outputs that recommend direct customer action without approval.
- Reject outputs that hide evidence or confidence.
- Reject outputs that claim legal or credit authority.
