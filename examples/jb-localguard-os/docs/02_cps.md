# 02 CPS

## Context
Relationship managers need to review local-business customer risk using scattered signals: account behavior, document status, policy constraints, and public context. In a competition demo, the product must show both AI usefulness and financial safety.

## Problem
RM teams lose time gathering evidence and may make inconsistent judgments. A generic AI chatbot cannot be trusted because it lacks approval gates, audit logs, and risk controls.

## Solution
JB LocalGuard OS provides an AI Agent workflow that gathers evidence, classifies urgency, drafts a recommended next action, requires RM approval, and records the decision trail.

## Non-Solutions
- No autonomous customer outreach.
- No real customer data in public demo.
- No black-box risk score without evidence.
- No legal or credit decision automation.

## Success Evidence
- Three golden cases produce correct recommendation class and explanation.
- Every recommendation includes evidence and confidence.
- Approval is required before any customer-facing next action.
- Audit log records input, judgment, approval, and outcome.
