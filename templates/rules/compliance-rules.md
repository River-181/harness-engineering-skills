# Compliance Rules

## Product Risk Controls

- Map each high-risk action to an owner and approval gate.
- Keep audit events for model judgment, human decision, and final action.
- Disclose privacy, security, copyright, hallucination, explainability, and responsibility risks.
- Fail closed when policy status, data permission, or evidence is unclear.
- Separate legal conclusions from engineering assumptions; mark legal review needed.
